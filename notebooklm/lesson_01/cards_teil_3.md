**CREATE ONLY VOCABULARY TRANSLATION FLASHCARDS from the table below.**

**Strict rules for flashcard generation:**

- Front side: German word EXACTLY as written in column "Deutsch" (keep article der/die/das for nouns).
- Back side: Russian translation from column "Русский" + example sentence from column "Пример".
- **ONE flashcard per row — no more, no less.**

- Total expected cards: exactly 24.
